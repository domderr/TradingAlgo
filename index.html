Core Areas

1. Research & Development

Quantitative research, strategy validation, market regime studies, asset allocation models, ETF research and options analytics.

2. Trading Tools Development

Development of trading tools, dashboards, scanners and analytical models using Python, Google Colab, TradeStation, EasyLanguage, TradingView and AI-assisted workflows.

3. Coaching

Training and mentoring for traders, developers and professionals interested in systematic trading, portfolio construction, EasyLanguage and AI for trading.

4. Publishing

Books, articles, research notes, market reports, white papers and educational content focused on algorithmic trading and quantitative market analysis.

5. Algorithmic Market View

A systematic view of financial markets based on quantitative models, market regime analysis, relative strength, volatility, breadth and portfolio signals.

The Mosaic Trading Framework connects research, development, market monitoring, coaching and publishing into one integrated quantitative ecosystem.
