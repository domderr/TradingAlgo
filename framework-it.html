Cosa Costruiamo Insieme

The objective is not to replace your judgment. It is to make your technical process explicit, measurable and usable at portfolio level, so you can understand what to buy, what to avoid, how much to allocate and when the model needs to change.

Universo di Investimento

Define the markets, stocks, ETFs, sectors or asset classes that match your strategy and constraints.

Logica di Selezione

Translate your indicators and technical criteria into rankings, filters and objective entry conditions.

Regole di Portafoglio

Set position sizing, diversification, rebalancing frequency, maximum exposure and risk controls.

Validazione Storica

Evaluate performance, drawdowns, turnover, benchmark comparison and sensitivity to market regimes.

Report di Monitoraggio

Create practical outputs that show current positions, changes, risk, benchmark context and model status.

Supporto Piattaforme

Implement research tools in Python, TradeStation, EasyLanguage, MultiCharts, TradingView or custom reporting workflows.

Esempi di Visioni Tecniche

The same framework can support different methodologies. The starting point is your way of reading the market; the work is to make that view systematic and testable.

Trend + Stabilita

Favor instruments with persistent trend behavior, stable participation and controlled portfolio overlap.

Momentum + Rotazione

Rank markets, sectors or stocks by relative strength and rotate capital toward the strongest areas.

Allocazione Attenta al Rischio

Combine signals with volatility, correlation, drawdown limits or hedge rules to manage total exposure.

Workflow di Collaborazione

A custom project normally starts with your market logic and ends with a documented, repeatable process that can be reviewed over time.
