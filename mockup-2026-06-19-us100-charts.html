NVDAJune 19 snapshot

How to read the chart

The main chart shows the asset behavior inside the Mosaic framework. It is designed to explain why a stock belongs, or does not belong, to the current portfolio.

Histograms at the bottom

Possible status values

Selected
The stock is currently part of the five-stock portfolio for the market.
Watchlist
The stock is close to selection quality and should be monitored for possible inclusion.
Rejected
The stock does not currently satisfy the required Mosaic conditions.
