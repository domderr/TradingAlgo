What is TradingAlgo Mosaic?

TradingAlgo Mosaic is a quantitative portfolio construction framework designed to identify and manage portfolios of stocks, ETFs, and international markets using objective, rules-based criteria.

TradingAlgo Mosaic

Unlike traditional stock picking approaches, Mosaic starts from a broad investment universe and systematically selects a concentrated portfolio based on two key principles:

Trend Strength

Capital is allocated to assets demonstrating persistent and stable trends.

Diversification Through Correlation

Assets are selected not only for their individual quality but also for their contribution to overall portfolio diversification.

The objective is to build portfolios capable of delivering attractive risk-adjusted returns while avoiding excessive concentration in highly correlated positions. This approach is consistent with the framework's stated objective of identifying and monitoring diversified portfolios through systematic analysis and disciplined execution.

How Does It Work?

The process is fully systematic:

  1. Market Selection: Mosaic analyzes hundreds of securities across different markets and sectors.
  2. Trend Evaluation: Each asset is ranked according to the quality and persistence of its trend.
  3. Correlation Analysis: Highly correlated securities are filtered to improve diversification.
  4. Portfolio Construction: The highest-ranked assets are combined into a concentrated portfolio.
  5. Weekly Review: Positions are reviewed periodically and updated only when the underlying conditions change.

The result is a dynamic portfolio that adapts to changing market conditions while maintaining a disciplined investment process.

What TradingAlgo Mosaic Is Not

TradingAlgo Mosaic is a quantitative research and portfolio analysis framework.

It is not:

  • Investment advice
  • A solicitation to buy or sell securities
  • A discretionary portfolio management service
  • A personalized recommendation engine
  • A signal service providing trading instructions
  • A guarantee of future performance

The framework generates systematic rankings and model portfolios based on predefined quantitative criteria such as trend strength, relative performance, and diversification. Any portfolio, ranking, or market view produced by the model is intended exclusively for research, educational, and informational purposes.

Users remain solely responsible for their own investment decisions and should independently evaluate the suitability and risks of any investment before taking action.

Past performance, backtests, simulations, and model results do not guarantee future outcomes and should not be interpreted as forecasts or promises of future return.
