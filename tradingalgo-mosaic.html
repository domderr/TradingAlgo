What is TradingAlgo Mosaic?

TradingAlgo Mosaic is a systematic portfolio research service for independent investors who want weekly model portfolios, transparent selection rules and risk-aware validation across global equity markets.

TradingAlgo Mosaic

Systematic Portfolio Research

Unlike traditional stock picking approaches, Mosaic starts from a broad investment universe and builds a standardized model portfolio through a repeatable research process. The five selected stocks are the output of the model, not discretionary trading calls.

Trend Strength

The model ranks assets by the quality and persistence of their trend under current market conditions.

Diversification Through Correlation

Assets are selected not only for their individual quality but also for their contribution to overall portfolio diversification.

The objective is to produce model portfolios that can be monitored, compared with a benchmark and reviewed through a disciplined process while avoiding excessive concentration in highly correlated positions.

Methodology Overview

The process is systematic and repeatable:

  1. Universe definition: Each market starts from a defined and comparable list of securities.
  2. Trend and stability ranking: Assets are ranked according to directional strength and stability.
  3. Correlation filter: Highly correlated securities are filtered to reduce redundant exposure.
  4. Model portfolio construction: The model combines selected assets into a concentrated weekly portfolio.
  5. Benchmark comparison: Results are monitored against the relevant market benchmark.
  6. Weekly review: Positions are reviewed and updated when the underlying model conditions change.

The result is a research portfolio that adapts to changing market conditions while preserving a disciplined and auditable investment process.

Who Is It For?

TradingAlgo Mosaic is designed for independent investors and professionals who actively review portfolio decisions and generally manage meaningful capital, typically USD/EUR 100,000 or more.

  • Private investors managing their own portfolio
  • Financial advisors
  • Asset managers
  • Family offices
  • Quantitative research teams

It is not designed for investors looking for short-term trading calls, guaranteed returns or personalized portfolio recommendations.

What TradingAlgo Mosaic Is Not

TradingAlgo Mosaic is a quantitative research and portfolio analysis framework.

It is not:

  • Investment advice
  • A solicitation to buy or sell securities
  • A discretionary portfolio management service
  • A personalized recommendation engine
  • A signal service providing trading instructions
  • A guarantee of future performance

The framework generates systematic rankings and model portfolios based on predefined quantitative criteria such as trend strength, relative performance, and diversification. Any portfolio, ranking, or market view produced by the model is intended exclusively for research, educational, and informational purposes.

Users remain solely responsible for their own investment decisions and should independently evaluate the suitability and risks of any investment before taking action.

Past performance, backtests, simulations, and model results do not guarantee future outcomes and should not be interpreted as forecasts or promises of future return.
