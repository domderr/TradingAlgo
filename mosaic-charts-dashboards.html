Charts & Stock Dashboards

How to use equity, drawdown, exposure and stock dashboards inside Mosaic reports.

Portfolio Charts

  • Equity curve: shows the historical portfolio path over time.
  • Drawdown: shows declines from previous equity peaks and helps evaluate historical risk.
  • Exposure: shows invested capital, hedge and net exposure behavior.

Stock Dashboards

Open a market report from the reserved area, then use the current positions and stock links to inspect the five portfolio names. The all-assets page inside each market folder provides updated charts for the broader stock universe.

Recommended Use

  • Use portfolio charts to understand Mosaic as a system.
  • Use stock dashboards to inspect the individual names currently selected.
  • Use all available stock charts to understand the wider opportunity set.
